Lice Don't Play Nice


Lice go back over 5 million years. Even Cleopatra had her own golden lice comb. The bottom line is they are here to stay. They're very picky preferring to die rather than select any other host than human. And they are so hearty that when threatened, they mutate. Today's "super lice" can withstand pecsticides leaving us no choice but to comb and pick each and every nit from our infested darlnig's head. Ugh.
